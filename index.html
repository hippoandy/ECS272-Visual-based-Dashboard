HP

Three Pokemon Clusters Plotted depend on Pairs of Variables.

The Combat Power (CP) is the total of a Pokemon's value of Attack, Defense, Speed, Speical Attack, and Special Defense.
This value is available as "Total" in the dataset. After preprocessed the data, the min. and max. value of "Total" are 180 and 720, respectively. CP coule be and indicator of a Pokemon's strength. CP defined three groups, which is called "cluster" in the title.

These three groups (clusters) are:
* CP between 180 - 360: Weaker
* CP between 361 - 540: Medium
* CP between 541 - 720: Strong
